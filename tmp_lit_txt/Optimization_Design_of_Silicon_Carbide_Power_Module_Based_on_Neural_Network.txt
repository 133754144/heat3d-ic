                In  reference  [6],  genetic  algorithm  is  used  to  design  the 
            layout of power modules with two chips and three terminals, 
            but genetic algorithm needs a large number of population and 
            takes up a lot of computing resources, and it is not suitable for 
            multi-chip power module. The paper [7] improves the paper [6] 
            by  using  the  nesting  method  of  genetic  algorithm  and 
            enumeration, which reduces the number of population and the 
            computational  difficulty,  but  only  considers  the  parasitic 
            parameters  of  the  module,  and  the  efficiency  of  genetic 
            algorithm is still limited. A multi-objective optimization tool, 
            PowerSynth,  was  created  in  reference  [8]  to  calculate  the 
            electro-thermal  coupling  of  layout  through  a  reduced-order

            method, which provides a more diverse starting point layout for 
            PowerSynth optimization through a synthetic circuit network 
            table.  In  the  layout  optimization  of  SIC  multi-chip  power 
            modules, it is necessary to reduce the parasitic inductance and 
            the  highest  junction  temperature,  is  to  focus  on  the  need  to 
            solve the problem. With the increase of power density, multi-
            chip becomes the trend. However, based on the layout design 
            method  in  the  previous  literature,  due  to  the  complexity  of 
            integrated  electrical  and  thermal  performance,  the  parasitic 
            parameters, maximum junction temperature, DBC size and chip 
            coordinates  are  not  simple  linear  relations,  and  the  finite 
            element  simulation  modeling  is  difficult,  simulation  time-
            consuming, difficult to be used in complex multi-chip power 
            modules. In this paper, an electro-thermal co-design method for 
            SiC multi-chip power modules is proposed. By training ANN, 
            the mapping between input and output parameters is obtained.

                                                                                                    accurate relationship between DBC size, chip spacing, parasitic 
                                                                                                    inductance and maximum junction temperature, the step size of 
                                                                                                    parametric simulation should be as small as possible, which 
                                                                                                    will lead to low simulation efficiency. Therefore, the method of 
                                                                                                    training artificial neural network is used that will get the target 
                                                                                                    value quickly.
